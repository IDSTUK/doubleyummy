We can’t get enough of it in our house at the moment! We’ve had a lovely summer of guests staying and it’s been a big hit with them to. Its just oats, mixed spice, finely grated carrot (I prefer it finely grated but it also works with a normal grate!) and sultanas or raisins all topped with some plain yoghurt. See my earlier post for more details. 

We eat ours with a spoon but the texture is think enough (excluding the yoghurt) for little ones to pick up for self feeding if you prefer.
