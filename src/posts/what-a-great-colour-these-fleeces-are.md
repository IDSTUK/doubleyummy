Meeting the sheep at Stithians Show today. 

The girls loved watching the carding and spinning of the fleece too.   
We’ve had such a great day as the girls and I braved our first agricultural show without a buggy (one carrier) and just a handbag!  
It was great to see so many exhibitors and different breeds.   
I feel so privileged to be able to give my children a strong connection to their farming heritage.   
I believe that knowing where your food comes from is really important and helps children as they grow up to make informed and responsible decisions when sourcing their food.
