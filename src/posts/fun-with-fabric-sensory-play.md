Exploring fabrics as today’s sensory play @craftbox\_helston whilst mummy made our initials out of fabric. Well started making them!  
You don’t need to have expensive sensory toys, here we chose a bright red sequinned fabric, a silky one with butterfly print and a couple of cotton pieces with colourful flowers and patterns.  
This activity provided visual and touch stimulation. The girls enjoyed the movement of the fabric and loved playing peepo. We use scarfs too at home. Different weight fabrics are good as they move differently eg when thrown in the air or waved around.
