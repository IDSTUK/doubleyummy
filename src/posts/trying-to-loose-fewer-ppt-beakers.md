Love it when you go out to a cafe and they have lots of highchairs (or two that aren’t being used!). 

I’ve been loosing cups and so my new trick is to mix the bottoms and tops of the beakers so that I remember they’re mine and take them home… fingers crossed it works. A twin n mum perk 😋

For lunch here they had unsalted peanut butter wrap, steamed carrot sticks, sticks of elemental cheese, (out of shot) chopped giant grapes, kiwi and sweet potato wedges. 

It may seem like a lots of variety but a little more out of necessity than anything else as only small amounts of everything left 😋 both girls eat better with different things to choose from so win win.
