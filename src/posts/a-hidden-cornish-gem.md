This is the view from Terry’s sculpture park. What a talented man. 

We appreciate his generosity in sharing his passion through creating this free to visit sculpture park with a spectacular view. Another great excuse to get outside for some active family fun. 

​

[https://doubleyummy.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/img\_3522.mp4](https://doubleyummy.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/img_3522.mp4)

​  
Two swifts. So cute to see W and her cousin play around this sculpture chatting away. I think this was the last of this artists pieces and it seemed so fitting that one of his simplest pieces was being enjoyed (in their own way) by our own little artists. 

We love this local sculpture park which is just along the cliff path from Coverack (a charming fishing village for those of you who don’t know the area). The girls (well we all) really enjoy time outside exploring and this is somewhere that we’ll be coming more often.
