Little Miss Independent loves to help. Here she is enjoying shutting the gate in today’s beautiful sunshine 😍☀️💖 

Finding little ‘jobs’ for toddlers can help grow their self confidence and they love the encouragement and praise.   
My two love helping to carry things like the washing basket, helping to load/ unload the washing machine & brushing up (I don’t know where they get it from 😂). 

I’m thinking of getting them one of those handheld hoovers rather than a toy one 😋
