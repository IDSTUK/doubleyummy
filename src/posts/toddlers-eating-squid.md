Squid is a great low fat source of protein. But best of all cooked right it’s absolutely delicious!

Leonie & Neil thank you for such a delicious Mother’s Day lunch (at The Greenhouse). We all loved it. 

W & I did a swap as she ate nearly half of my squid so I finished off her delicious roast beef! 

My hubby and I often do a half and half when eating out but this is the first time I’ve done it with a toddler! I started by giving W little bits of the squid but then she picked up a large piece and gnawed her way through it.   
These new molars that are pushing through (3 out of 4 traumatically through) are making a big difference to what she can chew.   
Sometimes it’s easy to compare children’s abilities, including eating abilities based on age but different levels of dentation can make a big difference to chewing abilities.
