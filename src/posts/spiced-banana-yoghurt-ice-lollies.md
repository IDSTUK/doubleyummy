Ingredients:

Mashed banana 

Plain Greek yoghurt (or milk free alternative if needed)

Mixed spice or cinnamon   
Method:

Mix about 50:50 banana and yoghurt with a shake of cinnamon. Spoon into ice lolly mounds and pop into the freezer. 

When trying to take them out of the mouldy run quickly under hot water.   
The girls loved making these (with a little help!) and eating them 😍  
Yummy yummy yummy!
