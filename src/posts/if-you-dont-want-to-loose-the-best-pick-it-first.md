Feeling very philosophical this morning 😍

Happy 1st of September & ☀️ start of the Cornish indian summer to you. 

We’re out foraging or more specifically blackberry picking and I can’t believe how big and juicy they are this year. No exaggeration I’ve git some that are 2-3 times bigger than normal. They’re not cultivated just amazing! 

The girls love eating them as we go, as a snack or as part of a meal eg with our morning porridge. 

Free food that’s delicious and gets you more active is a win in my book. I’ve been treating it as a mini workout too 😋 by holding in my abs and reaching for the extra high (well high for me!) ones.
