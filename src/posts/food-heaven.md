Yesterday at the Porthleven Food Festival. So many passionate food entrepreneurs selling delicious food, talented chefs doing demos (& tasters!) and great live music.   
Apparently we were part of 25,000 people that went yesterday!  
The girls (&I) enjoyed the #idlerocks chef’s watercress and oyster veloute and salmon, as well as the crab mousse tortellini!  
We also loved the perfectly cooked scotch eggs. I’ve not found any as good as the ones at Borough Market until now 😍 we served thus with some wild mushroom and Parma ham macaroni and a mega falafel salad as a picnic in the family field.   
The girls also loved dancing to the music 🎶💃🏻💃🏻💃🏻🕺🏻

My one mistake though was not taking some extra fruit for the girls as just before we left W saw another little one with a banana and I felt guilty when I realised I’d not brought them any ‘proper’ fruit. Luckily I did have an emergency little box of raisins which did the trick for long enough so that we could get home to an apple! 😂
