Happy Good Friday everyone. 

Before you grab yet another chocolate egg to give as a present this Easter think. Could you give something that provides entertainment or lasting enjoyment instead? We were so pleased to receive these stain glass painting sets 🐣  
With an Easter egg that comes with 2 cream eggs providing 1,414kcal (about the same amount as the average daily energy requirement for a 4-5 year old girl!) and even just a 90g bag of mini eggs providing 444kcal it’s really worth think before you buy. Don’t get me wrong everything is fine in moderation, but moderation is the key! You’ll need to do a lot of Easter egg hunts to burn those extra calories off!  
I hope you’ve got a nice weekend planned. We’re off cockle picking (trigging) 😍
