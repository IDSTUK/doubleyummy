Swishy swashy, swishy swashy! 

Initially both girls weren’t sure about pushing through the grasses but after going into the spiral path behind me they happily led the way out! It’s amazing to watch as their confidence grows. 

We had such fun at this park in Falmouth- loads to see and do. 

Had to strip them down before getting in the car though as they watched an older boy jump in a big puddle and try to catch the splashes and of course they wanted to have a go at that 😂 I had their waterproof trousers in the car but had thought it would just be a really quick play before we headed on..!
