Sunshine after the rain, blue skies dododada (or however that song goes 😋) #lunch today for my #10monthold girls with tiger bread tuna and mayo sandwiches with sunshine ray satsuma slices (halved as easier to eat like that) and sweetcorn and sultana rain drops.

At this age babies are developing their pincer grips as so little pieces like this are great to help with this. My girls are loving little sandwiches too at the moment 😍

Seeing the shape of the tiger bread called out clouds to me this morning whilst preparing lunch! 🌦
