A perfect early spring Sunday activity in the garden. 

What do you like to do outside? 

Today we were finding daisies and bright yellow dandelions and counting their petals (well the number of pinches of petals!). The girls loved picking them and then enjoyed watching the petals drift to the ground. 

I just hope I’ve not taught my two to go out and pull all of the petals off my flowers now..! 

I used to love making daisy chains and I’m looking forward to teaching B&W to make their own. That’ll not be for a couple of years yet though!
