Well to be honest there isn’t an age! 

I started giving banana mashed and then in fingers (if you gently squeeze a banana it will split into three) which are easy to grab. 

When their hands were bigger I moved onto 1/3-1/2 banana depending on the size of the fruit. 

Now (17 months) if it’s a very small banana then I’ll give them the whole thing with it half peeled. If it’s a bit bigger I eat some before giving it to them to help with portion control 😋
