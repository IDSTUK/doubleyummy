Yummy mummy watermelon & feta salad with strawberries, olives and a balsamic dressing. 

### Ingredients

Red cos lettuce (or crunchy lettuce of your choice) – a handful, chopped

Round lettuce – a handful, chopped

Rocket – a handful

Cherry tomatoes – 3 cut into quarters

Strawberry – 1 large, cut into slices

Watermelon – a chunk cut into bite sized pieces

Olives – 4 olives sliced

Feta – 30g crumbled

**Dressing ingredients:**

1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar

1 tablespoon red wine vinegar 

4 tablespoons olive oil

### Method

Wash the salad veg and strawberry. 

Toss all of the ingredients together. 

Mix up the dressing by adding the vinegars to the oil and shaking. There is more dressing than you need but it keeps well so you’ve got it for later in the week. All that’s left is to enjoy this tasty salad. 

Serve with a slice of your favourite bread. I love seeded bread 🍞

This meal came about as I have yummy stuff in for the girls snacks  & I really wanted the freshness of a salad without it being boring. 

Verdict = double yummy 😍😋

The combination of fruitiness with the vinegar and the savoury and salty notes from the feta and olives give a delicious balance. 🍓🍅
