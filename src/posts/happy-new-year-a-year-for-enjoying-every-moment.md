Celebrating with some play dough fun 😀

These ice cube trays used to have a very different function for celebrating the coming of a new year 😂

We had fun with biscuit cutters, ice cube trays, coloured bowls & the texture of the back of the chopping board. What do your little ones like to use with play dough?

B decided she’d mix using her garden fork & W struggled with the concept of making shapes and not eating them. We all approach things differently but that’s what makes the world such a magical place.

Thank you to my family & friends (new and old) for all of your love and support throughout 2016. It’s been more appreciated than you know. Thank you also to the virtual community with your support with my new social media project – it’s bringing me more pleasure than I’d ever have imagined..!

Do you have goals, targets or resolutions for the year ahead? I’m going to try to make the most of every moment, especially those with my gorgeous girls.

May 2017 be a healthy and fun filled year of happiness for you all 😀🎉💖
