Keeping those sour tastes going in. 

We started on sour and bitter tastes at the start of the ‘introducing solids’ journey and now try to keep them going in regularly. 

Dare I say it but so far they both love these sharp tastes- long may it last. 

Since they’ve been eating I’ve been eating with them which has reduced my salt and sugar intake too. Great for us all.
