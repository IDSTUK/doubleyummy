Getting involved with meal prep. B&W have just turned 3 and love getting involved with the cooking, serving and niceties of mealtimes. Today they wanted to grate their own cheese. To be honest I didn’t think they’d manage it but after a quick prep talk about being careful not to grate yourself and the angle to hold the grater they were both successful and probably made less mess than me 😂

It made them much more interested in their soup (leftover roasted veggies inc quite a bit of pumpkin and some low salt stock blitzed together).
