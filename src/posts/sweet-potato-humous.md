One to try this weekend 😍

1 Sweet potato boiled

½ Carrot grated

4 Cherry tomatoes

½ Garlic clove

Small bunch basil

Juice of half a lemon

Olive oil to make consistency you’re after

Blitz together  
Enjoy as a dip as part of a salad, with veggie sticks, low salt bread sticks or rice cakes or in a wrap / pitta / sandwich or simply on its own.
