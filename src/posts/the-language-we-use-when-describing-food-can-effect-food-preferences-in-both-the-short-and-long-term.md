Love it (carrots for B and red cabbage for W), like it (potatoes and opposite veg), learning it (lamb and leeks).

We always try to avoid saying that anything is disliked- if you’re not keen or simply not tried it before or has if often it’s a learning it food. I try to have something that they really like / love and a couple if like it foods especially when having a learning it food. Previously leek has been one that neither of them were keen on and roast lamb is something we only have rarely so two learning it foods today.
