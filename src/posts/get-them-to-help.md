Fish cakes – being made by my little helpers. Get your little ones to help with food prep is a great way to help to encourage them to try new foods, but it doesn’t have to be something new that they help with!   
My girls love nothing more than to be helpful 😍 sometimes I forget and they’re moaning hanging onto my legs as I try to do something… and then I remember: get them to help!
