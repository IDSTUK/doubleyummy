​  
Did you know that under 5s should have at least 3 hours of active time each day? ​​​

​Wellies, coats, hats and gloves on & we’re off! Just because it’s a cold snap doesn’t mean you need to be stuck indoors. 

The girls loved exploring the garden before lunch. Getting out also helped me to reach my step goal and resulted in a happier family all round 😀
