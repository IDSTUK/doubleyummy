Balancing life with a swim with my baby girls at the weekend at Trelowarren. 

They (& I) love being in the water so much and get so excited kicking and smiling. 

It’s moments like these that we must treasure and when we have a more challenging moment try to bring these times to the forefront of our mind. 

My hubby had them both asleep before this and I got a ‘proper’ swim. Keeping fit and strong is such an important part of balancing life and doing it in such enjoyable surroundings is amazing. Keeping strong not only means I’m more able to play with and look after my girls but it also means that I burn up more energy when sitting or sleeping as muscle tissue has much higher energy requirements than fat tissue even when you are at rest 😍
