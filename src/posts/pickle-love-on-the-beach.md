Happy Father’s Day 💙 Here are our little pickles with their daddy enjoy the evening in the beach.   
Where else would you want to have tea?!   
We popped to the beach to meet some old friend’s who are back from Sydney. Some things in life change a lot but some things feel like it was only yesterday 😍  
All a bit last minute with the beach picnic so we had soft cheese sandwiches, cucumber, apple slices, chunks of cheese and pickled onions. The girls love pickles of all kinds which I’m pleased about as I LOVE pickles!  
This tea provides a portion of dairy, a portion of starchy carbs & 3 portions of fruit and veg.
