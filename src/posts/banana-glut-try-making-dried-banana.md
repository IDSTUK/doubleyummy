Bananas all about to go over together? Try making dried banana: Slice bananas and dry / dehydrate at 65’C for 6-7 hours. As dried fruit is relatively high in sugar and can get stuck in teeth it’s recommend that these as with all dried fruit are eaten at a meal time for general health dried fruit count once a day as a portion of fruit and veg and unlike most similarly sweet options they contain lots of fibre.
