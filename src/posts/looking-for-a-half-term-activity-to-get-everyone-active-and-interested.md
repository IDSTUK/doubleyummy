How about a walk to collect flowers to press?

Our girls loved finding the different the leaves and flowers which encouraged them to walk without asking to be carried 😀, they enjoyed arranging them on the page and then standing on the large books to start the pressing process.

We used blotting paper but any parchment should work well or even just normal paper (I’d avoid putting in inside a beloved book if you’re doing the latter in case any moisture escapes).

This activity was a great chance to be a little more tuned into what’s around us and a great learning & sensory opportunity with all of the different colours, shapes, textures and smell that the flora and fauna give us at this time of year.
