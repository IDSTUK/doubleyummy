Strawberry porridge 3 ways – enjoying yesterday’s pick your own adventure 🍓💖 

Strawberries are not only delicious at this time of year but they’re packaged full of the antioxidant vitamin C & dietary fibre. 

Fibre is really important for bowel and gut health. Here the combination of oats (starchy carbohydrate, soluble fibre & proven to be beneficial in controlling cholesterol levels), and milk & yoghurt which are great sources of calcium and protein. 

Calcium requirements are relatively high for under 1s (525mg/day) for more info on calcium requirements and sources see https://www.bda.uk.com/foodfacts/Calcium.pdf
