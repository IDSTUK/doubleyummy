Growing your own is such a great way to inspire children (& adults!) to get more from their food & to try new things. We have a out of hand parsley bush in the greenhouse which I planted a few years ago as a companion plant for the veggies in there. The girls love picking it to cook with.

Don’t worry if you’ are too late or don’t have suave to grow from seed as you can pick up loads of little plants at the moment.
