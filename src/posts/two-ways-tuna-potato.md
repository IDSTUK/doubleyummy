Serving the same thing two ways adds variety with minimal faff! Great for us as my girls love variety and I dislike too much faff 😋  
This is a bit of a use up left overs meal. Here we have broccoli (steamed), new potatoes (1/2 with a little sund

ried tomato pesto and 1/2 with creme fraiche), tuna (1/2 with creme fraiche) and strawberries.  
You could easily swap the creme fraiche for yoghurt or mayo. We had an open tub from making moussaka and I was keen to give the girls the sour taste. Lots of finger friendly foods here.
