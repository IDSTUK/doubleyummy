Child (and adult!) friendly nice creams. No added sugar and no sweetners and an ‘ice cream’..? Really?

Just mash up a banana add some yoghurt (we used Greek full fat) and add some mixed spice. Mix together and freeze in lolly moulds. The ratio of fruit to yoghurt doesn’t matter, these are about 50:50. You can use add fruit you like but I’d suggest a smoother one like banana, peach, mango etc as the ‘base’. We love peach and raspberry (use frozen raspberries at this time of year to keep cost and air miles down) – this recipe is #milkfree too so good for anyone needing to follow a free from diet.

These two crack me up with their chatter to each other 😂 Hurray for the sunshine ☀
