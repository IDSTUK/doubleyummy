Simple but yummy lunch: ham sandwich with a twist. 

Instead of putting butter or any sauce on the bread I spread some avocado on one slice and cream cheese on the other & to make it extra special (and extra tasty!) I used sundried tomato and olive bread.  
Verdict = delicious 😍☀️

The girls had the same as me on a smaller scale (!) and cut into little bits. They loved it too. 

We had chopped up tomatoes and chunks of streamed Apple on the side. Great for today’s picnic lunch.

Although avocado is higher in calories than other vegetables it’s rich in healthy fats and is lower in calories compared to mayo and butter so makes a great & tasty substitute.
